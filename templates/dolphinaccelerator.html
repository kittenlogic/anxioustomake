Dolphin Accelerator

Description

Dolphin Accelerator was commissioned by Temporary Art Review for Common Field Convening 2016 | Miami from October 23-25, 2016. The project was embedded in an essay by theorist and curator Joshua Simon published on temporaryartreview.com and on Common Field | Research and was part of a broader segment on the impacts of accelerated capitalism.
